About Me and My Research


Hi! I'm Xin Chen, a 4th year Ph.D. candidate in Pharmaceutics in UW SOP. My research interest lies in Drug Metabolism and Pharmacokinetics (DMPK), with special focus on transporters and pregnancy pharmacology.

I work on transporters (e.g., P-gp, BCRP) expressed in the syncytiotrophoblast using in vitro and in vivo models. In specific, I have been investigating the interaction between cannabinoids and transporters, which will be highly valuable for better understanding and prediction of potential drug-drug interactions during pregnancy. Recently, I am working on the regulation role of gut microbiome and cytochrome P450s.

I'm supervised by Dr. Qingcheng Mao (dec'd) and Dr. Jashvant D. Unadkat
